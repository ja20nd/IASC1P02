About the Author: Joel Antony

My name is Joel Antony. I am a fourth-year business administration/marketing student with a passion for the film industry. On this site, I write and curate my film reviews. I also have a passion for photography and film-making. I have always been interested in all aspects of film-making and I hope to be able to work on the production of a film or television show one day. The two aspects that speak to me the most is both directing and cinematography. I have always admired a director's ability to express and control their creative vision and manage such a large team of people to execute that vision. Similarly, I admire the technical skill required to be a cinematographer and I am fascinated by how the greatest cinematographers have such an in-depth understanding of their equipment, their subject and their environment. My favouite directors include Martin Scorcese, Christopher Nolan and Quentin Tarantino.
